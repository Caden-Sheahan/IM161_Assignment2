Fast Facts

  1. The Triforce of Courage is carried by LINK, the Hero of Hyrule, and the protagonist of the Legend of Zelda series.
  2. The Dragon (pictured to the right) can be found in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, and is named Farosh.
  3. Farosh's name is a reference to the Golden Goddess Farore, who was one of the three Goddesses who created Hyrule.
  4. Link is often the Hero who saves Princess Zelda in each of the games, Zelda posessing the Triforce of WISDOM.
