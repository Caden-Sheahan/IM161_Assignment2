Fast Facts - Triforce of Wisdom

  1. The Triforce of Wisdom is carried by ZELDA, the Princess of Hyrule, and often a wielder of magic.
  2. The Dragon (pictured to the right) can be found in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, and is named Naydra.
  3. Naydra's name is a reference to the Golden Goddess Nayru, who was one of the three Goddesses who created Hyrule.
  4. In Breath of the Wild, Zelda has the power to seal away GANON, who posesses the Triforce of POWER.
