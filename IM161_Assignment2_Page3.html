Fast Facts - Triforce of Power

  1. The Triforce of Power is carried by GANON, who is sometimes called Ganondorf when in his Gerudo form.
  2. The Dragon (pictured to the right) can be found in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, and is named Dinraal.
  3. Dinraal's name is a reference to the Golden Goddess Din, who was one of the three Goddesses who created Hyrule.
  4. In the Legend of Zelda Franchise, GANON is usually defeated by Link, the carrier of the Triforce of COURAGE.
